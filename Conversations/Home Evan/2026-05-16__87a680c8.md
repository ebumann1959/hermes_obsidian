The UI stays the same — you still have scene setting, state sliders, inject text. But instead of generating dialogue, it just broadcasts directions into the world and the characters respond.

**The sr-mc-bridge.py can be simplified or retired** — it was needed to puppet bots. Now bots puppet themselves.
